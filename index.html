VeriScore Frameworks

The assessment is built around six frameworks that shape your readiness profile, maturity signal and business gowth intelligence.

01

Business Structure & Governance

Measures ownership clarity, decision flow, accountability, internal controls, and whether the business can function beyond founder dependency.

02

Financial Discipline

Reviews cashflow visibility, record keeping, budgeting behaviour, pricing discipline, and the strength of financial controls.

03

Operations & Process Discipline

Evaluates repeatability, workflow clarity, delivery consistency, fulfilment strength, and the reliability of execution systems.

04

Market & Sales Execution

Assesses customer clarity, market positioning, conversion systems, retention strength, and the ability to grow predictably.

05

Compliance & Trust Readiness

Checks registration, governance hygiene, documentation quality, statutory readiness, and trust signals needed for scale and partnerships.

06

People, Digital & Growth Readiness

Measures team coordination, capability depth, digital adoption, scalability posture, and readiness for continuous business scoring.
