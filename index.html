VeriScore Frameworks

The assessment is built around six business frameworks that shape your maturity signal, readiness profile, and continuous scoring path.

01

Business Structure & Governance

Measures ownership clarity, decision flow, accountability, basic controls, and whether the business can operate beyond founder dependency.

02

Financial Discipline

Reviews cashflow visibility, record keeping, budgeting behaviour, pricing discipline, and the strength of your business financial controls.

03

Operations & Process Discipline

Evaluates repeatability, workflow clarity, fulfilment consistency, delivery systems, and the reliability of your internal execution engine.

04

Market & Sales Execution

Assesses customer clarity, market positioning, conversion systems, retention strength, and your ability to grow predictably.

05

Compliance & Trust Readiness

Checks registration, documentation, governance hygiene, statutory readiness, and the trust signals needed for partnerships and scale.

06

People, Digital & Growth Readiness

Measures capability depth, team coordination, digital adoption, scalability posture, and readiness for continuous scoring and expansion.
